## What is EcoItems?

EcoItems is a Minecraft plugin that adds fully customizable weapons, tools, armor, foods, and items to your server, each defined in its own config file. You decide the display, the attributes, the recipe, and the behaviour, so an item can be anything from a simple crafting ingredient to a piece of god gear with custom effects.

## What sets EcoItems apart?

- **Driven by the eco effects system:** attach the same effects, triggers, and conditions used across every eco plugin, no code required.
- **Craftable items:** give any item a shaped or shapeless recipe, with an optional crafting permission.
- **Custom foods and tools:** set nutrition, eat time, potion effects, mining speed, and per-block rules.
- **Rarity system:** categorize items by rarity with lore tags and weights.
- **Community library:** import and export items straight from lrcdb in-game.
